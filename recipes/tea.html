Tea recipe

Description

"Chai" is the Hindi word for tea, derived from the Chinese word "cha". In English-speaking countries, it typically refers to masala chai (spiced tea), a popular Indian beverage made from black tea, milk, a blend of aromatic spices, and a sweetener.

INGREDIENTS

  • water
  • sugar
  • tea leaves
  • milk

Directions

  1. Combine water,sugar together in a pot; cover and boil for 5 minutes.
  2. Remove from the heat, add tea leaves, and allow to steep for 10 minutes. Stir milk into tea mixture and bring to a boil. Strain tea into tea cups.
